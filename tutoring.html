Acing the SAT (or other test) begins with knowing the material, but equally important is your understanding of the test itself.

In an initial consultation, I will learn your strengths and weaknesses in the skill set necessary to the test you want help with, and we will decide if you wish to work with an "official" study guide or rely on me to design and provide the lessons.

The math section tests your ability to recognize math facts, because you will need to answer several of the questions in a few seconds in order to have time to work the multi-step problems. A strong grasp of math facts is the only way to do get your best score.

The reading section tests specific skills, while the writing section requires that you understand what the scorers are looking for and adjust your writing accordingly.
